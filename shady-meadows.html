Defect log

All bugs found (sorted by risk score)

ID Bug Area P I Score Severity
#1 Room calendar ignores single clicks On the reservation page, clicking a single date does not change the selection. Users must drag across dates. No feedback is shown when a click fails. Reservation 5 3 15 Very high
#2 Contact submit enabled when empty The contact form Submit button is active with no fields filled and fires a POST to /api/message, returning HTTP 400. Contact 4 3 12 High
#3 Check-in accepts past dates The Check In field has no minimum date constraint. Past days and months are selectable in the datepicker. Booking 4 3 12 High
#4 Check-out before check-in allowed The Check Out datepicker has no disabled days. Users can select a checkout date earlier than check-in. Booking 3 4 12 High
#5 No client-side validation (console 400 errors) Invalid form submissions reach the server and log Failed to load resource: 400 errors in the browser console instead of being blocked client-side. Global 4 2 8 Medium
#6 Booking form errors don't name the field Reservation form validation shows generic messages like "size must be between 3 and 18" and "must not be empty" without identifying which field failed. Reservation 4 2 8 Medium
#7 Booking nav anchor obscured by sticky header Clicking "Booking" scrolls to #booking but the small availability form sits under the sticky nav; "Our Rooms" dominates the viewport instead. Navigation 4 2 8 Medium
#8 Calendar allows past months Both homepage and room calendars let users navigate to previous months and select dates in the past. Booking 3 2 6 Medium
#9 Phone field accepts letters The contact phone field accepts alphabetic input (e.g. "abcdefghijklmno") and submits successfully. Contact 3 2 6 Medium
#10 Name field accepts numbers The contact name field accepts numeric-only input (e.g. "12345678") and the success message echoes it unescaped. Contact 3 2 6 Medium
#11 Amenities nav link goes nowhere The header "Amenities" link points to /#amenities but no element with id="amenities" exists on the page. Navigation 3 2 6 Medium
#12 Logout button visible when not logged in The admin panel displays a Logout button in the navigation while the login form is shown and no session exists. Admin 2 2 4 Low
#13 Footer quick links use href="#" Home, Rooms, Booking, and Contact links in the footer point to "#" and do not navigate anywhere. Footer 3 1 3 Low
#14 Footer social links use href="#" Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter icon links all point to "#" with no external URLs. Footer 3 1 3 Low
#15 Logout redirects to homepage Clicking Logout on the admin login screen redirects to the public homepage (/) instead of staying on or returning to the login view. Admin 2 1 2 Low
#16 Contact errors duplicated and unordered Contact form validation lists all field errors at once in a single block. Duplicated messages appear and errors are not grouped by field. Contact 2 1 2 Low
